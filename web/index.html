Spaceship Journey

Click the game first, then use keys or on-screen controls (on touch devices).

Music won’t play when the game is opened as a file.

In Terminal, run:
cd "SpaceshipJourney/web"
python3 -m http.server 8000 Then in your browser open: http://localhost:8000

Controls

Keyboard / PC

W A S D or ↑↓←→ — Move

Space — Shoot (direction you face)

Q or E — Dodge (barrel roll)

Shift — Speed boost

F double-tap — Helper fairy (+2 hits, 2 uses per game)

Touch / Mobile

On-screen: D-pad (move), FIRE, DODGE, BOOST. Double-tap fairy icon (top-right) for +2 hits. Tap FIRE to continue between rounds.

Goal

10 rounds, 90 seconds each. Reach the kill target before time runs out and before you take too many hits.

R1: 15 kills / 15 hits · R2: 20 / 15 · R3: 20 / 10 · R4: 25 / 10 · R5: 30 / 10 · R6: 30 / 5 · R7–8: 30 / 3 · R9: 30 / 2 · R10: 30 / 1

Beat all 10 rounds to win.
